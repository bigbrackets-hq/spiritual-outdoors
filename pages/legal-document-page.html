Privacy Policy

At Spiritual Excursion Pvt. Ltd., we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized services on our website. This cookie policy explains the types of cookies we use and your choices regarding them when you visit the website, inquire about them, or book your trip and get services from us.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit our website and are essential for a smooth and efficient experience, we use them to enhance user experience, optimize website performance, personalize services, and simplify booking. They help the site remember your preferences for future visits.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: It is essential for the site to work and allow basic navigation, booking, and access to secure the site. It includes login authentication and session management.
  • Performance Cookies: These are to help us collect data and analyze website usage to improve user functionality.
  • Functionality Cookies: This is to remember your choices (like language, region, and saved itineraries) for a personalized experience.
  • Targeting Cookies: These are used to display relevant ads and personalized offers based on your interests.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies for:

  • Advertising: Partnering with ad networks to show relevant ads.
  • Analytics: Identifying the use by utilizing third-party applications.

Managing Cookies

You can manage, control, or disable cookies via your browser settings to view, block, or delete them, but some features may not function properly when blocking or deleting cookies and may impact your experience on the site.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Please review it periodically.

Cancellation Policy:

  • A written notice of cancellation must be submitted by the client and acknowledged by the company.
  • Spiritual Excursion will fully refund all deposits if the cancellation letter is received no later than one month prior to the arranged trip start date.
  • Cancellation charges are expressed hereafter as a percentage of the total tour price.
  • If the cancellation is made before 30 days from the start of the trip the processing charge of Nepal Rastra Bank will be deducted but the remainder of the balance will be refunded.
  • If the cancellation is made before 15 days prior to the trip start date 30% of the total trip charge and Nepal Rastra Bank processing charge will be held and the remainder will be refunded.
  • If cancellation is made before 7 days prior to the trip start date 40% of the total trip charge and Nepal Rastra Bank processing charge will be held and the remainder will be refunded.
  • If cancellation is made within the week of your departure from Kathmandu no refund will be provided.
